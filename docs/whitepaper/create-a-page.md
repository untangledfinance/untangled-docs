## Participants
Key participants to the protocol include: 
Credit Investor: also called Liquidity Provider - individual/businesses who provides liquidity to a “strategy” i.e. individual asset pools or a diversified pool 
Issuer: a permissioned parties who, as the pool owner, issues pool tokens to credit investors in exchange for stable coins
Originator: originates credit assets like loans or invoices e.g., fintech lenders in the real world
Debtors: or end-borrower - individual/business that obtains finance from Originators. They may not have an on-chain presence and they typically repay directly into a designated account (collection account or locked box) off-chain 
Pool Admin: underwrites the pool by setting the key parameters such as collateral asset type, min first loss, debt ceiling   
Validator: independent party who validates collateral before them being used for asset-backed lending on-chain
Issuer and Originator collectively represent the borrowing side whereas credit investors represent the lending side.
